Submit Tax Exempt Paperwork


If you wish to claim tax exemption, please submit the information below for unshipped orders & future orders on this account:

The email associated with your E-Rigging account is [email]. Please use the same email in the form below.

Tax Exemption Process Notes:

  • We can only remove tax from orders that have not shipped and not been billed.
  • We will collect your tax exemption paperwork, and submit it to our third-party tax verification service. Upon verification your account will be setup so that you aren't charged tax for future orders made with this account. We estimate this process to take less than 24 business hours.
  • You must use your account tied to your email address ([email]) in order not to be charged tax.
  • You can setup multiple billing and shipping addresses for a single account, but if you change accounts / email addresses you must re-submit paperwork.
